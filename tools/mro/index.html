Criticality still matters after the calculation.

Demand history alone is weak evidence for insurance spares, long-lead assemblies, and parts that protect safety, environment, or major throughput. Validate item identity, interchangeability, preservation, supplier risk, repair route, and the consequence of a stockout.

Use the tools as a transparent baseline. Apply an approved critical-spares policy when consequence or lead-time risk lies outside normal demand behavior.
