Privacy at ReliabilityBench.

Effective date: July 22, 2026. This policy explains what information ReliabilityBench processes when you visit reliabilitybench.com or contact us.

ReliabilityBench is a static informational website. The calculators run in your browser; values entered into calculator fields are not submitted to, stored by, or transmitted to ReliabilityBench through the calculator itself.

Like most websites, technical information may be processed by service providers to deliver the site, including IP address, browser and device information, requested pages, approximate location derived from IP, referrer, and time of access. GitHub Pages, Cloudflare, and related infrastructure may process this information under their own terms and privacy notices to provide hosting, security, and content delivery.

Google Analytics 4

ReliabilityBench uses Google Analytics 4 with Measurement ID G-6Z73P5MMNS to understand site use, such as pages viewed, engagement, broad device/browser characteristics, and traffic source. You can limit analytics through browser privacy settings, cookie controls, content blockers, or Google’s available opt-out tools. Blocking analytics will not prevent the core calculator functions from working. Where consent is required by applicable law, analytics should be enabled only in accordance with the consent mechanism applicable to the visitor’s location.

We do not sell personal information. Information may be processed by hosting, analytics, email, security, or legal service providers acting for the purposes described above, or disclosed when required to comply with law, protect rights, investigate abuse, or maintain site security.

No internet service can guarantee absolute security. We use reasonable operational measures appropriate to this small informational service, but visitors should avoid transmitting sensitive data through the contact channel.
