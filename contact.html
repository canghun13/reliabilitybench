Contact

Send a useful correction or tool request.

ReliabilityBench welcomes practical feedback from people who use the calculations in industrial work.

How to reach us

Email hello@reliabilitybench.com for calculator feedback, formula corrections, content suggestions, or a broken-page report. Please include the page URL, the input values or scenario that produced the issue, and the expected result where possible.

This is not an emergency-support channel. Do not use it to report urgent plant, process-safety, or equipment conditions; follow your organization’s operational escalation procedures instead.

What not to send

Please do not email personal information, confidential production data, passwords, controlled technical information, customer data, export-controlled information, or details that your employer does not permit you to share. Email is not an appropriate channel for sensitive operational information.

Scope of responses

We may use aggregate, non-confidential feedback to improve a formula, explanation, or user experience. A reply does not create an engineering, professional, client, or advisory relationship, and cannot validate a site-specific decision.
