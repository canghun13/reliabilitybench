Field notes
Purpose of the workbench

Built for the discussion behind the number.

ReliabilityBench is for reliability engineers, planners, maintenance supervisors, production leaders, asset managers, and MRO teams who need a compact calculation while work is moving. It does not replace a CMMS, a detailed reliability study, or financial review.

The site is deliberately organized around decision boundaries. A failure rate does not guarantee mission reliability; availability does not include every logistics delay; a reorder point does not settle criticality. The method and limitation remain visible so the team can decide what deserves deeper analysis.

Operator check

  1. Use a shared definition for the event and time basis.
  2. Keep units and population scope consistent.
  3. Ask which action becomes justified if the result is true.
