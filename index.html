Planned Tool Areas

Four engineering workstreams.

The site will combine calculators, formulas, practical examples, references, and decision-support tools rather than publishing isolated KPI calculators.

01 / Reliability

Reliability Engineering

MTBF, MTTR, MTTF, availability, system reliability, mission reliability, and failure probability tools.

02 / Maintenance

Maintenance Performance

OEE, schedule compliance, preventive maintenance compliance, backlog, wrench time, and maintenance KPIs.

03 / Economics

Asset & Downtime Economics

Downtime cost, lost production, repair-versus-replace, maintenance ROI, and asset cost decision tools.

04 / MRO

MRO Spare Parts Planning

Reorder points, safety stock, EOQ, stockout cost, critical spares, and service-level planning.
