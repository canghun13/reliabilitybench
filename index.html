Why this workbench exists

Maintenance metrics are not decisions by themselves.

MTBF does not tell a planner whether a spare is available. OEE does not tell an asset manager whether replacement is economic. A useful engineering calculation makes the boundary visible: the data used, the formula applied, and the operational decision it can—and cannot—support.

ReliabilityBench is designed for reliability engineers, planners, maintenance supervisors, production leaders, and asset managers who need a compact, legible check while work is moving. Inputs use explicit units, invalid math is stopped at the source, and every result is paired with practical interpretation.

Use a calculation responsibly

  1. Match the asset population, duty cycle, and time window.
  2. State what is included in a failure, repair, or downtime event.
  3. Test a result against operating reality before committing money or schedule.
