Maintenance KPI Formulas

Formula definitions are useful only when the numerator, denominator, event rule, and time boundary are agreed.

MetricFormulaUse with care
MTBFOperating time ÷ failuresRepairable assets; stable event rule.
MTTRActive repair time ÷ repairsState whether delays are excluded.
BacklogReady work hours ÷ weekly capacityExclude work not ready to schedule.
OEEAvailability × performance × qualityUse defensible ideal cycle time.
ROI(Benefit − recurring cost) ÷ investmentSimple annual screening metric.

Scope before comparison

Do not compare KPI values across sites until inclusion rules match. For example, contractor labor, planned outages, repeat failures, and production time can all change the story.
