Weibull Parameter Reference

Quick definitions for the two-parameter Weibull model and the reliability quantities derived from it.

TermMeaningFormula
ηCharacteristic life; reliability is 36.8% at η.Time unit
βShape; below 1 decreasing, above 1 increasing hazard.Dimensionless
R(t)Survival probability at time t.exp[−(t/η)^β]
h(t)Conditional failure intensity.(β/η)(t/η)^(β−1)

Fitting caution

Parameters depend on data quality, censoring treatment, population consistency, and failure-mode separation. Always attach a model scope and confidence statement to a published estimate.
