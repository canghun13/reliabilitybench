Formula reference with the boundary attached.

A formula is only as sound as the definition behind each term. This quick reference flags the central assumption for common tools; open the related calculator for input guidance and an example.

MeasureFormulaKey boundaryTool
MTBFTotal operating time ÷ failuresRepairable population and counted failuresMTBF
MTTRTotal active repair time ÷ repairsClarify whether logistics delay is excludedMTTR
ReliabilityR(t) = e−λtConstant, independent failure rateProbability
Series reliabilityRs = ∏RiAll elements required; independent failuresSeries
Parallel reliabilityRp = 1 − ∏(1−Ri)At least one path can succeed independentlyParallel
AvailabilityMTBF ÷ (MTBF + MTTR)Inherent availability only; no support delaysAvailability
OEEAvailability × performance × qualityConsistent planned-time and ideal-rate rulesOEE
Reorder pointAverage demand × lead time + safety stockDemand and lead time use matching unitsROP
Safety stockz × σdemand × √lead timeNormal-demand approximation; fixed lead timeSafety stock

Notation and units

Use one time unit throughout a calculation. A failure rate expressed per hour must be paired with a mission duration in hours. Currency outputs remain in the currency basis of the entered inputs; ReliabilityBench does not convert currency or apply tax, inflation, discount rates, or escalation unless stated.
